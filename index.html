Our Mission

We want to solve the issue of poor sanitation in Johannesburg, specifically in Makers Valley, by providing a solution that gives more people access to cleaner and safer toilets. While combating the sewage issue, we also aim to limit the chance of a cholera outbreak by using a composting toilet feature to produce fertilizer for plants. Additionally, it will use renewable energy to operate independently of load shedding and ensure consistent functionality.
