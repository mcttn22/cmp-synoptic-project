Our goal is to target two sustainability problems by trying to solve sanitation and food production issues. This is going to be targeted by our project of Smart Toilets, a public toilet block that composts waste. These toilets will be locked and opened by the public, decreasing the chance for vandalism and unsheltered homelessness. In addition, farmers will be able to collect the fertilized soil and use it to increase the nutrients of their yield. Hopefully, with due time, this will cut prices of food and help with the nutritional value.
