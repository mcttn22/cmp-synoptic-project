Our Mission

We want to solve the issue of poor sanitation in Johannesburg specifically in Makers Valley by providing a solution that will allow more people access to cleaner and safer toilets. While combatting the sewage issue, we also want to limit the chance of a cholera outbreak by using a composting toilet feature to produce fertiliser for plants. In addition to this it will use renewable energy to avoid the effects of load shedding.
